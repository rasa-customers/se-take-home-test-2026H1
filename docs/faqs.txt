Q: How long do I have to return an item?
A: You have up to 90 days from the date of purchase to return an item. Please make sure the item is in its original condition and packaging when you send it back.

Q: Do I need the original packaging to return or exchange an item?
A: Yes, items should be returned in their original packaging and condition whenever possible.

Q: Do I have to pay for return shipping when making an exchange or return?
A: No, you don’t need to pay for return shipping. We’ll provide you with a prepaid shipping label to send the item back.

Q: Is there a fee to return or exchange an item?
A: No, returns and exchanges are free as long as they’re completed within the 9-day return window.

Q: When will I receive my refund after returning an item?
A: Refunds are typically processed within 5 to 7 business days after we receive your returned item.

Q: Can I return an item I received as a gift?
A: Yes, gifted items can be returned or exchanged. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method or as store credit.
